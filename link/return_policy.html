Return Policy

At My Earningbd, our commitment is to foster transparency and reliability in every aspect of our platform. We recognize the significance of clear guidelines, particularly when it comes to financial transactions. Please take a moment to familiarize yourself with our refund policy regarding investments and subscription fees.

Investment Refunds
Unfortunately, we do not offer refunds on investments made through our platform as part of our policy. This includes funds utilized for various activities such as trading digital assets. We strongly advise all users to thoughtfully consider their investment decisions, exercising caution before committing funds to ensure informed choices.

Subscription Fee Refunds
Upon registration and account activation, a non-refundable subscription fee of 100 Taka is required. This fee serves to cover administrative costs, ensuring the security and integrity of our platform. Consequently, we are unable to provide refunds for this subscription fee once it has been paid.

However, it's essential to understand that our policy is crafted to maintain fairness and transparency for all users. While we apologize for any inconvenience this may cause, we remain steadfast in upholding our policy consistently for the benefit of the entire user community.
